About MS infra

We are committed to helping people affected by the earthquake.

As you might know, February 6th, 2023 there have been very violent earthquakes in Turkey that have caused a lot of damage. Thousands of people have died and lost everything in one day.

We want to collect money asap for the people who are now without a home, work, father, mother, brother, sister & food etc. due to the earthquake in Turkey. Many people have lost their relatives, lost their homes, lost their jobs, lost their children and so on. We have already sent drivers and professionals to help there, but we can use every little bit of help!

We want to be able to contribute from here by means of a fundraising campaign. We PERSONALLY GO TO TURKEY to deliver the money to the right addresses in order to afford clothes, food & such basic necessities for the people in dire need right now… every € counts.

Our Mission

Achieving Change

We are going to Turkey to ensure that the money ends up in good hands and that something is actually done!

Care & Protection

With the money collected we buy tents, lighting and heating for the people who no longer have a home.

Saving people

Rescuing people and children trapped under the rubble.

Peace On The Planet

Hopefully together we can help as many people as possible.

Increasing Awareness

Spreading locations of living people trapped or people in need of shelter!

Sharing Happiness

What we do comes from the heart the circumstances they are in are no one's favor.
